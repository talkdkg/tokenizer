don't like teeble hooks
By ptangler on December 20, 2013
I use the Kastmaster in medium to large sizes for light saltwater fishing. They cast amazingly well and the flash they give off attracts fish. The only thing I do not like is the teble hooks. The trebles are hard to get out of bluefishs' toothy mouths and they do a lot of damage to weakfish. they also snag more frequently if you try boucing the lure off the bottom. An appropiraite sized single hook will not lead to lot fish. I replace the standard hooks with single hooks dressed in buckskin..
